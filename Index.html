About Mogok

A beautiful city in Myanamar which locted in South East Asia. Mogok is renowned as the "Valley of Rubies" and is famous for producing some of the world's finest rubies, including the coveted "pigeon's blood" rubies. The region also yields high-quality sapphires, spinels, and other gemstones

Why Mogok is Famous?

Ruby: Mogok is famously known as the "Valley of Rubies." It's the source of the world-renowned "Pigeon's Blood" rubies, prized for their deep red color. Sapphire: The region also produces high-quality blue sapphires. Other Gemstones: Mogok is a treasure trove of various gemstones, including spinel, peridot, garnet, moonstone, and rare varieties like painite and hibbonite.
